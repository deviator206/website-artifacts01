About the Sculpture

Sculpture is the branch of the visual arts that operates in three dimensions and one of the plastic arts. Durable sculptural processes originally used carving (the removal of material) and modelling (the addition of material, as clay), in stone, metal, ceramics, wood and other materials but, since modernism, shifts in sculptural process led to an almost complete freedom of materials and process. A wide variety of materials may be worked by removal such as carving, assembled by welding or modelling, or molded, or cast.

Welcome to the beautiful world of art. It has been proved that art is an excellent way of distressing oneself by utilizing your spare time to explore your inner creativity. Age is no bar when it comes to art and craft.

Personally I see art as one’s own journey and mine began about 20 years ago. I started out with murals, fabric paintings, moulds, soft toys, fountains, chemical flowers, etc. I have added a lot more to my palette since then. Varli paintings, antique mirrors, brick carvings, marble dust murals and my personal favorite Tanjore paintings! One might not be familiar with few of them. I have mentioned details about these in the categories mentioned for better understanding. Apart from individual classes I conduct group workshops as well from children and women of all age.

If you are just looking for an appropriate gift for your friends, colleagues, relatives for special occasions or murals or other wall hangings instead you can place an order with us. We design bulk order gifts according to your budget using different materials. As a certified interior decorator I would love to help suggest artifacts, hangings, etc. according to your interiors. We welcome those who are interested in learning to make these artifacts or place an order.
